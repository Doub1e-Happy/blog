## Why a blog?

Every developer should have a blog. Not because it'll go viral — it probably won't. But because writing forces you to think clearly. You can't hand-wave when you're putting words on a page.

This blog is where I'll document things I learn, solutions to problems that took me too long to figure out, and occasional thoughts about software and technology.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Web development** — React, Next.js, TypeScript, CSS tricks
- **Tooling** — things that make development faster and more enjoyable
- **Architecture** — how to structure applications that don't collapse under their own weight
- **Random discoveries** — things I wish I'd known earlier
